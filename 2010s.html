The 2010s

Color

Color in the 2010s was often more subdued than in the 90s and 2000s, with a lot of pastel colors being used. The use of color was often more consistent, with a particular color palette being used throughout the website.

Typography

Typography in the 2010s was often more stylized than in the 90s, but less so than in the 2000s. The use of custom fonts and text effects was less common, and text was often left unstyled, with the default font and size being used.

Layout

Layout in the 2010s was often more structured than in the 90s and 2000s, with the use of grids and columns being more common. The use of active whitespace was also more common.

Imagery

High definition images became the norm and were often the focus on the page. High resolution video was sometimes used as an alternative.

Interactive elements

Interactive elements in the 2010s were often unstyled, with the exception of color. Links were often styled with color, and forms and fields were often left unstyled. Scrollbars were often unstyled, and the use of color to create visual hierarchy, interest, contrast, balance, and rhythm was often more common.
