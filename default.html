The 90s

Characterized by:

  • Bevel and emboss effects
  • Animated GIFs
  • Animated cursor
  • Colored icons
  • Cheesy colored box/drop shadows and borders
  • Bulleted lists
  • Unstyled links (except for color - red or blue)
  • Unstyled forms and fields
  • Unstyled scrollbars
  • Welcome text

Characterized by:

  • Bevel and emboss effects
  • Grainy images
  • Mix of sharp and slightly rounded corners, often asymmetric borders
  • Colored, low res icons
  • Harsh gradients
  • Unstyled links (except for color)
  • Unstyled forms and fields
  • Unstyled scrollbars
  • Liberal use of background images, often distractingly so

The 2010s

Characterized by:

  • Large, high res images
  • Newspaper and magazine layouts
  • Minimalist design
  • Oversized headers and footers

Characterized by:

  • Dynamic presentations
  • Responsive designs
