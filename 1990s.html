Welcome to the 90s!

    •

  • Color

    The use of color was often inconsistent and did not follow any particular pattern.

  • Typography

    Typography in the 90s was often unstyled, with the exception of color.

  • Layout

    Layout in the 90s was often unstructured, with a lot of bulleted lists and unstyled forms and fields.

  • Imagery

    Imagery in the 90s featured a lot of animated GIFs, colored icons, and clip art.

  • Interactive Elements

    Custom mouse cursors were commonplace.
