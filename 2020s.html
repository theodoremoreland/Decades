The 2020s

Web design in the 2020s is the age of dynamic presentations. Things such as text or images that appear as the user scrolls have become fairly common.

Color

Color in the 2020s is often more subdued than in the 90s and 2000s, with a lot of pastel colors being used. The use of color is often more consistent, with a particular color palette being used throughout the website.

Typography

Typography in the 2020s is often more stylized than in the 90s and 2000s, with the use of custom fonts and text effects being more common. Links are often styled with color, and text is often given a drop shadow or emboss effect.

Layout

Layout in the 2020s is often more structured than in the 90s and 2000s, with the use of grids and columns being more common. The use of whitespace is also more common, and the use of background images is often more subtle.

Interactive elements

Interactive elements in the 2020s are often more dynamic than in the 90s and 2000s, with the use of scrolling animations and other dynamic presentations being more common.
